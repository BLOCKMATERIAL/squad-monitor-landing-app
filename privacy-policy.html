Thank you for using Squad Queue Monitor. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

Squad Queue Monitor does not collect or transmit personal data. Our application operates entirely on your local machine and does not:

  • Connect to any servers
  • Transmit any user information
  • Track or monitor user behavior
  • Collect personal information
  • Access files unrelated to its operation

The application only captures screenshots of your game screen for the purpose of detecting queue status through OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology. These screenshots are processed locally and are not transmitted outside of your computer.

2. Local Storage

Squad Queue Monitor may store configuration settings on your local device to remember your preferences. This data never leaves your device and is only used to improve your experience with the application.

3. Third-Party Services

Our application does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or APIs that would collect information about you or your usage of the application.
